Peanut butter overnight oats

Description

quick and easy breakfast!

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup milk of choice
  • 3/4 tbsp chia seeds
  • 2 tbsp peanut butter, or other nut butter
  • 1 tbsp maple syrup
  • 1/2 cup oats

Instructions

  1. mix everything, and leave overnight!
  2. top with anything you desire :)
